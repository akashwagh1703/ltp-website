### Primary Colors
- **Primary Green:** `#00E676` - Bright turf green (main actions, highlights)
- **Secondary Green:** `#2E7D32` - Dark forest green (gradients, secondary elements)
- **Accent Orange:** `#FF6F00` - Vibrant orange (problems, urgency, warnings)

- **Light Text:** `#F8F9FA` - Off-white (primary text)
